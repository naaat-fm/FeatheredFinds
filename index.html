Goldcrests

This bird is teeny tiny, it’s one of the smallest birds in the UK! It's green and brown with a little yellow stripe on its head, like a tiny crown. The male has a bright orange spot in the middle of his crown.

Lesser Grey Shrike

This bird is black, white, and grey, and the male has a pink chest. He likes to live in warm places and eats lots of insects. He likes to sit on high things like telephone wires to look for insects.

Bee Eater (Merops apiaster)

This bird has a colourful, bright plumage! It loves to eat bees and wasps. It's so clever that it knows how to remove the sting before eating them, so it doesn't get hurt.
